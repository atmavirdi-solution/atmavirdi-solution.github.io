🌱 MycroWrap

Innovative eco-friendly cling wrap engineered to keep food fresh while protecting the planet.


  • Bio-Composite: Mushroom-derived chitosan + pineapple leaf fibers (PALF).
  • Antimicrobial: Inhibits 99.9% of bacteria naturally.
  • High Strength: Solves brittleness issues found in many bioplastics.
  • 100% Compostable: Breaks down within 60 days.
  • Circular Economy: Converts agricultural waste into premium sustainable packaging.
