Our Green Innovations

Atma Viridi Solution develops next-generation biomaterials and sustainable product concepts designed to reduce reliance on conventional plastics.

🌱 MycroWrap

An eco-friendly cling wrap concept designed to support food freshness while reducing environmental impact.


  • Bio-Composite Concept: Developed using mushroom-derived chitosan and pineapple leaf fiber reinforcement.
  • Natural Material Potential: Designed with naturally derived components that may support improved hygiene performance.
  • Improved Durability: Engineered to address brittleness challenges commonly found in early-stage bioplastics.
  • Compostability Goal: Designed with compostable material principles, with performance depending on testing and composting conditions.
  • Circular Economy Focus: Converts agricultural by-products into higher-value sustainable packaging concepts.

🥤 MycroSip

A next-generation eco-green straw concept developed as a sustainable alternative to traditional plastic straws.


  • Biodegradable material design approach
  • Plastic-reduction focused beverage solution
  • Food-contact conscious product development
  • Supports lower-waste green manufacturing
  • Designed for future zero-waste ecosystems
